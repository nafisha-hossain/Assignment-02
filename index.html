Movement

Embracing consistent movement is key to a healthier lifestyle. Whether it's a brisk walk, a dynamic workout, or daily stretches, keeping your body active promotes overall wellness. Incorporate varied movements to keep your routine engaging and effective.

Time

Making time for fitness is an investment in your future well-being. Prioritizing exercise in your daily schedule enhances both mental and physical health. Remember, even short, regular sessions can yield significant benefits.

Practice

Regular practice is essential for achieving fitness goals and building lasting habits. By committing to a consistent workout routine, you can improve strength, endurance, and flexibility over time. Stay dedicated and celebrate each milestone along the way.

Weight Loss

Effective weight loss combines balanced nutrition with regular exercise. By creating a calorie deficit through a healthy diet and active lifestyle, you can achieve sustainable results. Focus on gradual progress and maintain a positive mindset throughout your journey.

BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator is a tool used to estimate body fat based on a person's height and weight. It provides an easy way to assess whether an individual is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. The calculator works by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30 and above Obesity

Calculate your BMI

BMI calculator is a quick tool that can give you a general idea of your health status, but it's important to remember that BMI doesn't account for all factors, such as muscle mass or distribution of body fat.
